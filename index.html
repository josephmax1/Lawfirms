About Adrianos

Law Offices of Adrianos Facchetti Burbank Personal Injury Attorney was on the brink of being put into the foster care system at just three days old. Fortunately, a loving Argentinian and Brazilian couple decided to adopt him, lifting Adrianos out of a life of uncertainty.

Early Life and Education

When he was 12, Adrianos attended a private Catholic school managed by a principal who had a nasty habit of publicly shaming students. After witnessing this, he convinced his parents to let him switch to public school. He made an appointment with the principal, listing ways the school could improve, leaving a lasting impression.

Adrianos graduated from UCLA with a Bachelor's Degree in History with Departmental Honors and went on to attend Pepperdine University School of Law, where he served as the President of Phi Delta Phi, an honors society.

Career

In 2006, Adrianos founded The Law Offices of Adrianos Facchetti with a vision to create an extraordinary law firm grounded in justice and fairness. His first trial resulted in a major victory for his client, earning accolades for his closing argument.

He has successfully handled a variety of cases, including auto crashes, pedestrian and motorcycle accidents, and police misconduct.

Personal Life

Outside of work, Adrianos enjoys reading, mentoring colleagues, playing guitar, and spending time with his wife and two children, Gigi and Severin. He also provides Pro Bono adoption services through the Alliance for Children’s Rights.

Accolades

Adrianos is a member of the Consumer Attorneys Association of Los Angeles and was recognized in the 2014-2016 Southern California Super Lawyers Rising Stars List. He holds an AV Preeminent Rating from Martindale-Hubbell®, reflecting his strong legal ability and high ethical standards.

He also serves as a Transportation Commissioner for the City of Burbank, advising the city council on public funds and traffic safety initiatives.
